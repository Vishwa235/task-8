Mahatma Gandhi
The Father of the Nation, India

Leader of Indian Independence Movement


Mahatma Gandhi, known as the Father of the Nation in India, led the Indian independence movement against British rule. His philosophy of nonviolent resistance inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Philosopher of Nonviolence


Gandhi developed the concept of Satyagraha, or nonviolent resistance, which he used to lead successful campaigns for civil rights and social change. His teachings emphasize truth, nonviolence, and peaceful protest.
